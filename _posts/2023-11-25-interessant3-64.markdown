It is interesting to see the reduction in inequality over time in social market economies, and also to note that the top 1% in China receive a much larger share of income than France, and larger than the UK. 2. ** Remembering John Tooby:** Recently passed, John Tooby was a pioneering figure in evolutionary psychology. To give one example of his impact, his work on "Coalitional Instincts" delved into how group affiliations can impact individual rationality, particularly in ideologically united groups. The evolution of these instincts was crucial for our ancestors to successfully compete for resources, shifting power from solitary individuals to coordinated groups, however, because they drive individuals to express beliefs and opinions that affirm their identity within a group, they often lead to irrational or extreme beliefs.
