2.  **Understanding Ergodicity in Probability Theory**  
In a lecture at [Gresham College](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Gresham_College), Professor [Ole Peters](https://ergodicityeconomics.com/author/olebpeters/) explains the concept of ergodicity and its consequences on everything from science to betting. Covering the history of probability theory, this video provides a detailed explanation of how ergodicity helps us understand complex systems and make better decisions. **This is, in my view, one of the most mind-blowing videos on all of Youtube.**  
[Watch the lecture on ergodicity](https://youtu.be/f1vXAHGIpfc?feature=shared)
