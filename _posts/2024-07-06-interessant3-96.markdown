2.  **The Concept of Preference Falsification: Why We Lie About What We Want**  
Preference falsification is a social phenomenon where individuals hide their true preferences to conform to perceived social norms. This Wikipedia article explores how this behaviour impacts various aspects of society, from politics to personal relationships. Understanding preference falsification helps us see the hidden currents that shape public opinion and social change.
