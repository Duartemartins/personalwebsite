2.  **The Greater London Project: Embracing London's Potential**   
Joe Hill and Sam Bowman present a compelling vision of how London can be even greater. This summary excellently captures the unique benefits of London, from its global appeal and rich cultural scene to its innovative spirit. Highlighting these strengths, they outline ambitious plans to address challenges like insufficient housing and crumbling infrastructure. Their proposals include expanding public transport, pedestrianising key areas like Oxford Street, implementing dynamic congestion charges, and embracing new technologies such as driverless trains and drone deliveries. Their optimism is rooted in the belief that good ideas and committed individuals can drive the city's growth. Read the full vision [here](https://substack.com/home/post/p-146722392).
3.  **Exploring the Tells of the Near East**   
Tells are mounds created by many generations of people living and rebuilding on the same spot, layer upon layer. There are tens of thousands of archaeological Tells in the Near East, discovered by satellite but never opened. These sites hold untapped historical treasures that could reshape our understanding of ancient civilisations. Learn more about this archaeological phenomenon [here](https://x.com/lefineder/status/1815801881203204374).
