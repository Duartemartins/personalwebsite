###  Portugal's Dutch Disease: An Economic Dilemma

This study from the University of Coimbra dives into how international aid and EU structural funds have inadvertently led to economic imbalances in Portugal, resembling the effects of Dutch Disease. It discusses potential solutions, such as investment in infrastructure, to mitigate these adverse effects, offering insights into economic policy and strategy.
