### ** The Law of Triviality**

Why do committees spend more time discussing bike sheds than nuclear reactors? Enter _Parkinson’s Law of Triviality_, a delightful sociological observation that explains many baffling meetings and political speeches. The more complex a topic, the more people defer; the simpler it seems, the louder the opinions.

 [Wikipedia: Law of Triviality](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Law_of_triviality)
