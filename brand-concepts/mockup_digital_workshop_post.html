The Manifesto

Accelerate European Progress

By Duarte Martins • August 27, 2024 • 4 min read

Europe stands at a critical juncture. The stagnation of the last decade must be met with a radical commitment to technological acceleration. For too long, we have prioritized caution over creation, regulation over realization, and the status quo over the future.

The architecture of our progress has become brittle. We see it in the slow adoption of frontier technologies, the flight of our most ambitious founders to other shores, and a general malaise that suggests our best days are behind us. This is a choice, not a destiny.

"Stagnation is not a natural state for the continent that birthed the industrial revolution; it is a policy failure."

The Requirement for Velocity

To accelerate is to acknowledge that the risks of standing still are far greater than the risks of moving forward. We need a new "EU-ACC" framework that treats technological growth as a primary moral imperative. This means streamlining the path from research to market, incentivizing high-risk/high-reward ventures, and fostering a culture that celebrates the builder.

We must move beyond the "Brussels Effect" as our primary export. While regulation is necessary for a stable society, it should be the guardrail, not the engine. Our engine must be innovation.

A Call to Action

This is an open letter to the builders, the engineers, and the policy-makers who still believe in a vibrant European future. The tools are in our hands. The capital is available. All that is missing is the collective will to accelerate.
